A suspicious husband used his drone to catch his cheating wife getting into another man’s car.

A suspicious husband has used his drone to catch his wife hopping into another man’s car. 

The YouTuber, who goes by YAOG, says that his wife had "been getting called in early to work more often the past couple weeks, and then I got a call from someone telling me something was going on." 

He tried to follow her using conventional methods, but suspected she was getting "tipped off."

So he decided to pull out his drone and follow wife to a carpark where she appears to kiss a man and get in his car.

In a video posted to YouTube, YAOG shows the drone footage he captured, all while providing live commentary. "There's the guy, f--king a--hole, that my wife is cheating on me with. My wife of 18 years," he says. "And if you watch carefully, you're going to be able to see 18 years go right down the f--king drain."

"We had a good marriage," YAOG emotionally continues. "I thought we had a good marriage. Apparently not."

That's one way to catch them. Some commenters were quick to offer up suggestions on how he should confront her about the footage...

One commenter said: "You should change the locks and when she comes home and tries to get in, fly a drone down with divorce papers.""Future case study for divorce lawyers right here," another commenter wrote. "Use of drone footage to prove infidelity."

A third person offered a more violent solution: "Attach a bomb to the drone﻿."

Others wrote words of encouragement. "Dude your way to good for her you deserve way way better," one commenter said. Another added, "I don't know who you are or what you do for a living and I honestly don't care but here's the thing man... There is nothing you can do to change the past but you can do something about right now. Be the best you can be. Stay calm and be AWESOME! Peace out bro!﻿"
