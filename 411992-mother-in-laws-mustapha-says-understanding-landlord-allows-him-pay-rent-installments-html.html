Mother-In-Law actor Mustapha, real name Andrew Muthure, has revealed that he has been going through a hard time since the pandemic hit.

According to the actor, cost-cutting measures saw him lose his radio job and could only survive through the help of relatives and a few friends.

“I was on Hot 96 for five years and unfortunately, I was retrenched alongside a few others. That was very difficult," he said.

The veteran actor divulged that when he was fired from Hot 96, his wife was pregnant.

“My wife was pregnant at the time, she had a very difficult pregnancy and was on bed rest throughout the day. My severance package was all spent out on bills,” he narrated.

Mustapha revealed that his extended family has been supporting him through food donations and even paying rent.

He also lauded his landlord for being understanding and accepting rent payments in instalments.

"I have survived through prayer. My family members have been my heroes for they bring me food, help to pay rent, and offer encouragement. God has been with me all through. I have a very understanding landlady, and she knows how tough things are,” he said.

Friends go missing

Amidst his woes, Mustapha said that some of his friends stopped picking his calls.

“However, one or two of my friends would put in a good word for me,” said Mustapha.

To survive, the actor said that he planted crops to feed his family.

“I don’t have much I can call work, and I still live off my extended family. There are a few shillings that come from Mother In Law episodes that I did last year. I am diligently looking for work,” said the actor.

Fear

Mustapha further divulged that one of his biggest fears is dying broke.

“We have had lots of Kenyans celebrities who are just big on TV and end up dying paupers. That’s one of the fears I experienced when I got fired from radio,” he said.

He, however, expressed optimism that things will get better and he will realise his dream of opening a production studio.

“I was on the floor lying down, but now I am on my knees,” said the actor, who is also a voice-over artist.
