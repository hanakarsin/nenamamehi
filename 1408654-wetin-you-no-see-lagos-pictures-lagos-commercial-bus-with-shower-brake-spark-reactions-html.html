Wetin You No go See for Lagos: Pictures of Lagos Commercial Bus With Shower as Brake Spark Reactions

July 29, 2024
Post

- A Nigerian man has taken to social media to share an unusual car part he saw in a Lagos bus

- In the pictures, what should have been a brake was replaced by a showerhead

- Hilarious reactions trailed the man's photos as viewers shared pictures of similar weird body parts in commercial buses they'd boarded

Photos of a Lagos commercial bus popularly known as Danfo with a shower head as its brake clutch has generated reactions among Nigerians.

A man took to Twitter to share the weird bus body part.

@Davidchibike wrote:

"There is nothing you cannot see in Lagos, especially inside danfo."

In the images he shared, the driver applied the shower head like it was a normal car brake.
