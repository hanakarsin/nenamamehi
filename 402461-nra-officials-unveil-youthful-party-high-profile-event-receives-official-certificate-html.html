NRA officials unveil youthful party in high-profile event, receives official certificate

Post

- TUKO.co.ke understands NRA Party leaders hosted a huge event at Serena Hotel to unveil the youthful political party

- The political party also received its registration certificate during the same event

- NRA leaders disclosed they will soon roll out a nationwide recruitment to get more members

Gone are the days when all you could hear of was just Jubilee Party and Orange Democratic Movement (ODM).

Today, Kenya prides itself in being one of the few countries that support multi-party politics.

With that said, the National Reconstruction Alliance (NRA) a youthful political party recently cemented its foundation by officially unveiling the party.

TUKO.co.ke has learnt the fast-rising political party was officially unveiled on Thursday, February 11.

The unveiling ceremony was held at the Serena Hotel, Nairobi and saw the attendance of many people, including party leaders such as Amemba Magufuli, Riziki Dunstan, Fredrick Ojiambo and a host of other youthful political leaders who are on the verge of shaping the country’s political sphere.

Other leaders who attended the unveiling of NRA include Kuchio, Rumenya and Mundalo.

TUKO.co.ke understands the vibrant political party was launched in early 2021 but officiated the whole thing with its unveiling on the aforementioned date.

According to information reaching TUKO.co.ke’s news desk, the party was also handed its registration certificate during the unveiling ceremony in a move that inspired many young politicians looking forward to changing the county’s political narrative.

Speaking during the unveiling of NRA Party, one of the founders Dunstan Riziki confirmed it has not been an easy journey but the party is hopeful many youths with jump on board.

‘’We have 41 offices across the country, we hope to double the number by end of the year. I think the major struggle is recruitment of members. There is a lot of mistrust fear and dishonesty amongst our Kenyan people, we are a country of very few honest people.’’ Riziki said.

The unveiling of the NRA party came barely a fortnight after then party officials confirmed it was a registered political party, with youths being the highest priority.

As earlier reported by TUKO.co.ke, Riziki, Magufuli, Ojiambo and their team registered the National Reconstruction Alliance Party of Kenya (NRA) in a bid to prepare themselves for Post Uhuru succession.

NRA, whose Party headquarters is in Ngong- Matasia boasts of tens of fully functional offices in 41 counties which were approved by the Registrar of Political Parties Mrs Anne Nderitu.

According to official documents sighted by TUKO.co.ke, NRA has over 500, 000 registered members across the country, with many being the youth.

On what Next for NRA, Riziki a lawyer by profession said the party is prepared and that they have all systems ready for massive National recruitment exercise.
