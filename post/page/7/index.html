Judges, Politicians, Public Office Holders To Get 114% Salary Rise

July 24, 2024
Post
The Revenue Mobilisation, Allocation and Fiscal Commission (RMAFC) has announced a 114 per cent increase in the salaries of politicians, judicial and public office holders in Nigeria. The RMAFC chairman, Muhammad Shehu communicated the changes during the presentation of the reviewed remuneration package to the governor of Kebbi, Nasir Idris. The new salaries will be effective from January 1, 2023, said Shehu, represented by Federal Commissioner Rakiya Tanko-Ayuba. Shehu justified the decision based on the provisions in paragraph 32(d) of part 1 of the Third Schedule of the 1999 constitution of the federal government (as amended).
