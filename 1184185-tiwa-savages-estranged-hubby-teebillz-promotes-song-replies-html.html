Tiwa Savage's estranged hubby Teebillz promotes her new song, she replies

July 24, 2024
Post

- Tiwa Savage and her husband Teebillz appear to still be good friends

- This is after several rumours have made the rounds suggesting that they are no longer a couple

- Teebillz recently showed his support for Tiwa’s new single

It is still unclear as to whether popular Nigerian musician Tiwa Savage and her estranged husband Teebillz are back together but it’s obvious both of them are still friends.

A recent example is how Teebillz took to his Instagram page to share the cover art for Tiwa Savage’s latest single Lova Lova which features Duncan Mighty.

Touched by his gesture, Tiwa took to the comment section of the post to say thank you. See their exchange below:

This is coming after both Tiwa and Teebillz came together to celebrate their son’s birthday via Instagram. Teebillz wrote that “I see a little more of me in you with each passing year. To the world you’re JamJam but to mummy and I, You are OLAOLUWAKITAN You’re part of my strength.

I pray for incomparable wisdom and knowledge of God upon you. You’ve been a great source of happiness to mummy and I. I pray may you never stop making us happy in Jesus name.”

In response to his post, Tiwa savage recalled how her son's heart rate dropped when she welcomed him 3 years ago. Obviously emotional, Tiwa noted that her heart is full of praise and thanksgiving.
