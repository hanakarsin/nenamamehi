This Little Girl Went On Her First Run And She Is All Of Us

By Aimee Lutkin

Updated Oct. 14 2018, 12:20 p.m. ET

Do you remember the first time as a kid when you consciously exercised? Mostly kids just run and scream and push each other around for no reason, or because it's soccer practice, but at a certain point you become aware that people are moving their body for a reason and you could too. I remember my first "run" and it was horrible! And that's just what Twitter user Sirena Salazar's little sister discovered on her first run.

Salazar shared the Snapchat journey of her sis going on her first deliberate run ever, and it's quite a journey. Wearing a bright blue sports bra, black leggings, and orange sneakers, her sister definitely looked the part. Too bad she learned the hard way that running is not as fun as it seems. In other words, her exercise experience was relatable to everyone that has ever jogged when they are out of shape.
