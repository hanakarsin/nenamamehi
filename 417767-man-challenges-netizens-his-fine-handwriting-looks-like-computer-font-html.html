Man Challenges Netizens with His Fine Handwriting that Looks Like Computer Font

April 20, 2024
Post
  • A Nigerian man, Johnson Chima Okpala, has left many stunned as he showcased his handwriting
  • Johnson challenged anyone interested in a writing contest to come forward
  • Some social media users accepted the challenge and showed off their handwritten notes

A young man earned the admiration of social media users as he showed off his fine handwriting.

Johnson Chima Okpala challenged netizens who believe they have better handwriting come forwards.

The proud man wrote:

"If your handwriting can beat mine, come closer. Challenge."

Student's fine handwriting

In other news, a 13-year-old class seven pupil from St Mary's Tabaka Girls Primary School amazed online users with her neat handwriting.

The learner's well-organised type was not limited to her creativity as her choice of words in her composition titled "The great recovery" was also commendable.

According to a Facebook post in the Teachers of English group made by her trainer, Arthur Bells, on January 19, the learner had neatly organised the characters, which almost resembled the Gothic Book Hand handwriting.
