Matt Doherty, a 31-year-old fullback from the Republic of Ireland, is getting closer to being re-signed by Wolves in this summer transfer window after leaving Atletico Madrid, according to Teamtalk.

West Ham are interested in 28-year-old England midfielder James Ward-Prowse who Southampton valued at £40 million, according to Mail.

Micky van de Ven, a 22-year-old Dutch defender valued at £25 million, is anticipated to sign with Tottenham Hotspur, the Mirror reported.

Everton have expressed interest in Tom Heaton, a 37-year-old former England goaltender who now plays for Manchester United, the Mirror claimed.
