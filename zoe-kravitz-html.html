Zo Kravitz - Age, Family, Bio

Known for her roles in several popular films including X-Men: First Class, No Reservations, and The Brave One. She played Christina in the 2014 science fiction film Divergent and Toast the Knowing in the 2015 film Mad Max: Fury Road. In 2019, she was cast as Catwoman in the 2022 film The Batman. 

Before Fame

She attended the State University of New York at Purchase to study acting, but left after one year to pursue a career in acting. 

Trivia

Her father is musician Lenny Kravitz, who won four Grammy Awards from 1999 to 2002. 

Family Life

Her mother is Lisa Bonet, who became famous as Denise Kendall on The Cosby Show. She previously dated musician Twin Shadow and Penn Badgley.  She married Karl Glusman in 2019 and filed for divorce from Karl in December 2020. She's been romantically linked to Channing Tatum. In October 2023, it was announced that they were engaged.

Associated With

A distant cousin of hers is Al Roker, who became known as the weatherman on The Today Show.
