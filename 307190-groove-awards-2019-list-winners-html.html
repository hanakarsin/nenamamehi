Groove Awards 2019: List of all winners

March 30, 2024
Post

East Africa’s most prestigious and biggest gospel music awards ceremony, Groove Awards 2019, has finally released a full list of all its 14th edition winners at the Movenpick Hotel & Residences in Nairobi. All the categories were highly and tightly contested as they contained different top-notch artists in the country. So, who won in each category? How about who scooped the biggest awards?

Due to changes in the Gospel Music industry, Groove Awards 2019 introduced additional categories. Fans were also given a chance to nominate several musicians, unlike in the past where they couldn’t. This has made it easy for many artists to get awards. Here is a full list of Groove Awards winners 2019.

Groove Awards list of 2019 winners

In the awards Gala, Jabidi who had received impressive 11 Groove Awards 2019 nominations didn’t disappoint as he scooped 5 out of the eleven titles. He was followed by singer and TV presenter Timeless Noel and Moji Short Baba with each having six nominations. The two bagged one and four awards, respectively. Below are the winners of East Africa’s biggest gospel event. We have included Groove Awards nominees 2019 in each category to help you identify the tightly contested spots.

Groove Awards 2019 voting criteria

For the first time in history since Groove Awards was launched, public participation was involved so as to make stakeholders and the entire gospel industry happy. This gave fans a sense of ownership while participating in the Award’s phase and saw many new artists being nominated for the event.

Next, Groove panel performed a thorough review of all the 153 submissions that had been done by the public. With that, they came up with different categories before announcing nominees later.
