Family and friends of the late former president Daniel Arap Moi have gathered to commemorate the first anniversary of his death at Kabarak.

The late head of state passed away on February 4, 2020, while receiving treatment at The Nairobi Hospital.

President Uhuru Kenyatta was among the leaders who arrived at Mzee Moi's home to mark the first year since the long-serving president breathed his last.

Missing in action

However, conspicuously missing from the memorial service were former prime minister Raila Odinga and Deputy President William Ruto.

Some reports indicated the DP was not invited to the memorial service perhaps owing to the reigning political differences between the second command and Moi's son, Senator Gideon Moi.

Some of the leaders who attended the event were Amani National Congress (ANC) leader Musalia Mudavadi, Wiper leader Kalonzo Musyoka, Senator Moses Wetang'ula among others.

Supremacy battles in Rift Valley

In the recent past, supremacy battles between the deputy president and the Baringo senator have become more prominent as the two politicians seek to fill the void Mzee Moi left in the vote-rich Rift Valley region.

In early January 2021, a group of youths in Nandi county blocked the convoy of Gideon Moi from driving to Kapsisiywa to visit Talai elders.

The youths who got wind Moi was planning to visit the village ruined Moi's trip to the elders.

Gideon had been invited by Talai elders who are known for performing special rituals and blessing political leaders.

However, a fortnight ago, the Talai elders crowned Gideon Moi, as the Kalenjin community spokesperson on the wee hours of Friday, January 22.

The coronation, however, widened the rift between him and the DP who underwent a similar process in July 2020.

During his visit to Kapsisiywa, the DP was also crowned by the Talai clan and given a special baton as a sign of trust of his leadership by the Kalenjin community.

The elders clad Ruto with traditional attire synonymous of his stature before they proceeded tohand over a symbolic leadership stick which they said would act as a guide in his future political aspirations.
