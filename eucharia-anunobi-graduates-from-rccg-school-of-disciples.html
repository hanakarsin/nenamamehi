Eucharia Anunobi Graduates From RCCG School Of Disciples

April 23, 2024
Post

Eucharia Anonobi graduate from RCCG school of disciple. (Photo credit: Stargist.com)

Actress Eucharia Anunobi at no point in time allowed her lose of child to affect her, the actress of recent was seen going back into acting and also donating her child’s properties to the less privileged.

The actress recently graduated from the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) School of Disciples and took to her Instagram page to reveal the happening. She had this to say on her Instagram page,

“Beloved ones , join me and celebrate JESUS CHRIST , who saw me through the completion of the Redeemers School of Disciples course 2017 / 2018 session .
The devil wanted to stop me from finishing , for my son died while I still had four subjects / papers to sit for , but thanks to God who had promised that in these things am more than a conqueror ( Romans 8 : 37 ) .
I graduated with 900 ( nine hundred ) other disciples last Sunday 25th February 2018 .
Please help me hala : JESUS NA YOU BE OGA , JESUS NA YOU BE OGA ,ALL OTHER gods NA SO YEYE , EVERY OTHER god NA SO SO YEYE“.
