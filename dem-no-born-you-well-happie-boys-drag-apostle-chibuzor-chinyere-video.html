'Dem No Born You Well'

April 10, 2024
Post

Happie Boys, previously employed as security staff for a fast food outlet in Nigeria, have recently taken another swipe at the well-known clergyman, Apostle Chibuzor.

Several months ago, the group, who are now dancers, publicly criticized the cleric for taking them to Cyprus and allegedly leaving them to struggle without sponsoring their education.

During the initial dispute, Apostle Chibuzor offered to arrange return flights for the Happie Boys, but they chose to stay in Cyprus.

Now, a new video has surfaced, showing the Happie Boys rekindling their quarrel with the OPM pastor, as they hurl insults at him.

One of the group members is heard saying “dem no born you well” – a local expression indicating defiance and disrespect – when the topic of returning to Nigeria was brought up again.

The video, found on Instagram, reinvigorates the controversy surrounding the Happie Boys and their disagreement with Apostle Chibuzor.

Watch the video shared by an Instagram user @ tosinsilverdam below:

This is coming weeks after the cleric laid curses on the viral security guards sent to Cyprus on scholarship.

Apostle Chibuzor in an audio making the rounds online expressed displeasure over the attitude of the security guards who had leaked the details of their chat with him.

According to him, money used in sponsoring the boys to Cyrus was gotten from members’ tithes and offerings, however, the boys have decided to pay him back with evil.
