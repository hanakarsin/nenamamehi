Film and TV actor most remembered for his roles on the shows Roseanne, Moesha, and Welcome Back, Kotter, among many others. He played Mandingo in the 2007 film Sublime.

Before Fame

He briefly attended Wilkes University in Pennsylvania before starting his professional career.

Trivia

He worked as a back-up singer, creating vocals for Rick James' 1981 album, Street Songs.

Family Life

He was born to Hilton and Clothilda Jacobs and was one of nine children.

Associated With

He once appeared on the sitcom Moesha, which starred Brandy as the title character.
