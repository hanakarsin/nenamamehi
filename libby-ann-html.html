Social media star and content creator who is best recognized as one half of the hunnaxlib TikTok account. She and her fiancé Hunter post fitness, challenge, and comedy content. Their Instagram account named hunnaxlib_ gained over 1 million followers on the video platform. 

Before Fame

She launched her TikTok account in May 2020. She played D1 volleyball at the University of Wisconsin Green-Bay and graduated with an art degree major and a graphic design minor.

She and Hunter post-challenge and vlog videos to their Hunna & Lib YouTube channel every week. One of her most popular TikToks features a wedding prank. It has gained over 18 million views.

She has a younger brother named Anthony. She was born in Appleton, Wisconsin. She began a relationship with Hunter Crist in 2019. They got engaged in July 2023.
