Rapper Falz Kisses The Leg Of Mystery Lady As They Step Out On A Date

May 15, 2024
Post

Nigerian Rapper, Folarin Falana popularly known as FalztheBahdGuy has hinted at finding love with his latest post on social media.

Naija News reports that the political activist took to his Instagram page to share a video of himself and a mystery lady stepping out on a date.

In the video, Falz could be seen helping the lady to wear her sandals and later gave her a kiss on her leg.

Although, he didn’t show the face of the lady, the rapper captioned the post: “Date night #Gentleman #GentlemanDoings”

15th Headies: BNXN Wins Next Rated Category

Meanwhile, Nigerian Singer, Daniel Benson popularly called Buju or BNXN, has emerged as the winner of the Headies’ Next Rated Award.

BNXN was nominated alongside Ayra Starr, Zinoleesky, Lojay, Ruger and Portable.

However, Portable got disqualified from the award, following his death threat to other nominees, on the ground of losing the award.

BNXN on Monday emerged as the winner of the category and goes home with the 2022 Bentley Bentayga reportedly worth over N300 million.
