A friend and associate of Nigerian Instagram celebrity cum International fraudster, Ramon Abbas also known as Hushpuppi, has taken to social media to celebrate the social media sensation on his 40th birthday.

Naija News reports that Hushpuppi, turned 40, today, 11th October, in prison.

Celebrating the embattled internet sensation, his friend Sikiru also known as Pac penned a heartfelt note to him to mark the special day, noting that he was writing from a place of hope and pain in his heart.

He added that as Hushpuppi’s verdict is approaching, he believes that he will be freed and they would reunite.

Pac also shared a picture of Hushpuppi and a video of the two of them driving around town together when he was still a free man.

In his words; ”Dear Rahman, I write you from a place of hope and pain, the hope that as your verdict draws close and a pronouncement kick starts, our reunion is made possible, and the pain that comes with every day knowing that you are in there.

“Rahman, it’s your third birthday behind guard bars, I pray that you continue to find strength, and I pray for peace From within, that powers you into greatness. Happy Birthday, Blood. Forever Faithful.”
