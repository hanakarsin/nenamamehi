State house spokesperson Kanze Dena has fondly revealed the names of her three children.

Dena shared a video of a song by Tanzanian gospel artiste Christina Shusho saying that the song, one of her favourite tunes, always guides her to pray for her children.

The names of her three jewels are Nathaniel, Eli, and Joe.
