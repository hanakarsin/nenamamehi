Dennis Itumbi says sorry for lying to Kenyans Ruto's wife donated Omo to starving Turkana residents

July 06, 2024
Post

- Itumbi apologised for the misleading images he shared showing Rachel Ruto distributing Omo and other items

- He also confirmed the deputy president's wife was in fact not in Turkana as he had alleged

- The said photos had grabbed attention of Kenyans who quickly noticed the images were old

- They also wondered why Mama Rachel would be donating Omo to starving Kenyans in a drought-stricken region

State House Secretary of Innovation, Digital and Diaspora Communication, Dennis Itumbi, has been on the receiving end since he shared a five-year-old photo of Deputy President William Ruto's wife allegedly donating detergents to starving Kenyans in Turkana county.

Itumbi was forced to apologise after Kenyans busted him for trying to mislead them that Mama Rachel Ruto had gone to the drought-stricken Turkana to donate food and Omo, a popular washing powder.

The vocal blogger took to his Twitter page to say sorry for misleading the whole nation and in the process badly embarrassing the DP's wife who is known to be very humble and prayerful.

"I apologise for images I shared here earlier. Mama Rachel Ruto was not in Turkana. I relied on unverified images. This was my mistake and I am sorry. I have since deleted them. May the record reflect the true position and my apology too. Jioni poa (good evening," he tweeted, albeit after some thorough bashing.

Kenyans who for a moment believed Mama Rachel was in Turkana to supply Omo quickly discovered the photos shared by Itumbi and which painted the DP's wife in bad picture were in fact five years old, as one could clearly see they were dated April 2014.

The netizens also came to the correct conclusion that Rachen was nowhere near Turkana on the date she was alleged to had travelled to the poverty-stricken county to distribute the much needed relief food and needless washing powder.

Rachel confirmed through her own Twitter page that she was indeed not in Turkana as was being suggested as she was attending the Africa House of Prayer Conference at the Catholic University of East Africa on that particular day.

"I briefly attended the 28th Africa House of Prayer Conference at the Catholic University of East Africa. I was so blessed to fellowship and pray! Prayer is indeed the way to go! We thank God for this great initiative," she tweeted without commenting on the viral photos shared by Itumbi.
