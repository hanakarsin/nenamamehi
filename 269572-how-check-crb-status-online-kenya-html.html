How to check CRB status online in Kenya: guide and options

A positive CRB rating means that you consistently pay loans and debts on time, while a negative indicates that you may have defaulted paying them. Kenya's lenders can determine if you are a defaulter by accessing the CRB listing record. Being blacklisted affects your credit score and can hinder you from getting loans and other types of credit from lenders. Learn how to check CRB status online in Kenya today.

1. How to check CRB status through Metropol

Metropol is an information and credit management company. It was established in Kenya in 1996 and is licensed by the Central Bank of Kenya to provide Credit Reference Bureau (CRB) Services to all banks in Kenya. The company also began providing credit rating services to the public in 2006.

Metropol can access the CRB checklist and inform you if you are on it. You can check your CRB status via the official Metropol website or app. Metropol Crystobol app is downloadable from Google Play Store and App Store. Please follow these steps to check your CRB status online:

To get a reference number for signing into the Metropol website:

Below are the CRB check online steps for those who want to use the TransUnion website:

  • Visit the TransUnion website.
  • Scroll down to "Know your score. Protect your credit."
  • Click on "Get started."
  • Fill out the form with personal details, mobile number, email, and address.
  • Click on "I Accept & Continue to Step 2" and follow the prompts to get your credit score report.
  • Your Credit report contains your CRB Status details.

How to check CRB status via TransUnion mobile app

  • Download the TransUnion Nipashe mobile app from Google Play Store or App Store.
  • Provide your ID number and other personal details to register on the app.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to access your CRB status.

How to check your CRB through SMS?

  • Text your full national ID name to 21272.
  • Pay KES 50 registration fee to Safaricom Paybill number 212121 and your national ID number as the account number.
  • Request your credit status, and you will get the information in seconds.

How to check CRB status for free?

You cannot check CRB status for free in Kenya. Metropol, CreditInfo, TransUnion, and other companies that provide this service will charge you a small fee in Kenyan shillings.

How long does it take to get removed from CRB?

It takes one to three business days to get a CRB Clearance Certificate.
